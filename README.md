# Izzy Goes East

> *Belonging isn't about knowing the right words. It's about showing up.*

Izzy is half-Filipino, half-English, and not sure she's enough of either. When her mum plans a three-week trip from London to Manila, Tokyo, and Taipei, Izzy expects awkward family dinners and tourist photos. Instead, she gets a journal, a cousin who sees right through her, a grandmother who tells stories that rearrange the world, and a slow, funny, sometimes painful discovery that belonging isn't about knowing the right words. It's about showing up.
