## July 7th, Friday
### Jiufen, All Day

We took a day trip to this mountain town called Jiufen.

It's famous because it MIGHT have inspired a Studio Ghibli movie called Spirited Away. (Dad said actually it didn't officially inspire it but everyone says it did so basically it did.)

It looks like a movie anyway. Steep narrow streets, red lanterns everywhere, old buildings stacked on a hillside overlooking the ocean.

It was crowded with tourists but I didn't care. It was too beautiful to care.

We climbed so many stairs. My legs are going to be very muscular by the time I get home.

At the top there's this famous teahouse where you can sit and drink tea and look at the view.

We sat there for an hour. Just... being.

The tea was bitter but in a good way. The view was mountains and ocean and clouds.

Mum was quiet.

"You okay?" I asked.

"Just thinking. About how beautiful the world is. How much of it I haven't seen yet."

"Are you sad?"

"No. Grateful. That I get to see this. That I get to show it to you."
