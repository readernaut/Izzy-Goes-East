## July 9th, Sunday
### Last Full Day in Taipei

We spent the day just hanging out.

Auntie Grace made us breakfast. We watched a Taiwanese movie on TV. We went to another night market (because why not).

Sofia and Lily have become best friends somehow. They have inside jokes now that I don't understand.

At one point Mum and Auntie Grace were looking at old photos on Auntie Grace's laptop, laughing at pictures from university.

"You looked so young," I said.

"We WERE young," Mum said. "Younger than you can imagine. We thought we knew everything."

"Did you?"

"Absolutely not."

They laughed.

### Packing, 10:00 PM

Tomorrow we fly back to Manila for one night, then to London.

I've been away from home for almost three weeks.

Longest I've ever been anywhere.

**Things I have learned:**

1. How to say thank you in three languages (sort of)
2. How to ride a jeepney
3. How to eat with chopsticks (better than before at least)
4. How to light incense
5. How to order from a vending machine
6. How to be okay not understanding everything
7. That family is bigger than I thought
8. That I belong to more places than I knew

**Things I still don't know:**

1. What I am, exactly
2. Where home is, exactly
3. Who I'm supposed to be

But maybe those are lifetime questions, not trip questions.
